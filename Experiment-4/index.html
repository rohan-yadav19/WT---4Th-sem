About Us

TiAmo is a neighborhood café and coffee shop in Biratnagar serving locally roasted coffees along with sandwiches, salads, fresh-squeezed juices, smoothies, and sweet & savory pastries.

  • Many cafés also serve a variety of teas, hot chocolate, and other non-caffeinated options.
  • From classic espresso to creative latte concoctions, we offer a vast selection of coffee beverages.
  • Pair your beverages with delectable pastries, cakes, sandwiches, or light meals, freshly prepared daily.
